Battleships On The Blockchain

You get a Shipcoin, and you get a Shipcoin!

Free Tokens

Earn Shipcoins by playing. These are actual NEP5 tokens that will be tradable on a $NAS DEX (once one is created). There will only ever be 1,000,000 Shipcoins $SHC.

Playing is like participating in an airdrop or claiming tokens from a faucet, but way more fun.

On The Blockchain!

This game runs on a decentralized game server powered by Nebulas. Every game is public and verifiable, the game server is always available, and cheaters will never win.

Educational

This is open-source and even includes a whitepaper. Take a look to understand how this works and to see how concepts presented here could be extended to other games.

How To Play

Check out the video above to see the game in action.
  • Get the NAS wallet extension.
  • Get some $NAS, free from GiveMeNas.com.
    • You'll need less than 0.000006 $nas (~$0.00004) to play.
  • Hit Play Now and then layout your board.
  • Each move you make will require a separate NAS transaction.
  • Follow the instructions on screen and do not refresh the page.

Prizes

You get a Shipcoin, and you get a Shipcoin!
  • Winner gets the 'Next Payout'.
    • The next payout grows by .1% each game.
  • Losers get 1% for playing out the game.
  • If you timeout, your opponent gets 10%.
    • And you get nothing, nobody likes a quitter.
  • Cheaters will be forced to timeout.
  • Once all 1,000,000 tokens have been won, that's it. GG.
