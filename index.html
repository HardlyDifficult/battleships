Battleships Game

You get a Shipcoin, and you get a Shipcoin!

Free Tokens!

Free to play and the winner gets Shipcoins, these are actual NEP20 tokens that will be tradable on a $NAS DEX (once one is created). There will only ever be 1,000,000 Shipcoins $SHC. Playing is like participating in an airdrop or claiming tokens from a faucet, but way more fun.

  • Winner gets 100% of the 'Next Payout'.
  • Losers get 1% of the 'Next Payout'.
  • If you timeout, you get nothing and your opponent gets 10%.
  • Cheaters will be forced to timeout.

Uncheatable

Cheaters will never win, either they lose the game or we detect the cheat and they lose the game.

Educational

This is open-source and even includes a whitepaper. Take a look to see how concepts presented here could be extended to other games.
